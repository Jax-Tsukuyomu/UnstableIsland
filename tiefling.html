The King

King Mendax, rules over The Petrum Urbs of Erebos, land of the mischivious tricksters, known for good deeds that come in a price, and unkown mischief. The king is not known for his strength in battle, but for his wit of trade, not having much to offer when it comes to land makes it an easy place to defend, and when you help everyone and harm everyone it's hard to make enemies. The king is constantly balancing between hated and loved. When he is hated he is protected by the Royal Knights of Erebos. nine cunning and swift fey'ri warriors always surrounding him and his wife Queen Petrum. the Fey'ri out of sight, waiting ahead and behing wherever there king is going and has been.



Fey'ri Warriors

The Warriors are not the only fey'ri in The Petrum urbs of Erebos but they are the strongest and most cunning. shrowded by the shadows they hide and wait to strike. most of the time they are purly for defense, but a little gentle persuasion and necissary every now and then.




History

The Half Orc Viking Canibles

We were the first on the island, or so we think. we only recently started recording our happenings again after countless libraries have burned to the ground...paper is oh so flamible and our kingdom burns hotter than the pride in which we walk. we have been here along with the native half orcs and a small village of humans just north of our Glorious Kingdom. The Petrum Urbs of Erebos, for several generations...but should the half-orcs really count as a society? They are more like animals, causing a riot for now reason, not even a smile as they tear through the flesh of their captures. Wonderful cooks though, honestly. We have a small arrangment with them, we supply them with various raw foods, and they trade us their golds and metals, the things they think are shiny and neat, how adorable. They have no hunger for us and we have no care for anyone besides ourselves. fair trade if I do say so myself.
